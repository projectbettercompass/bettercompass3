How can I obtain the information required to log in?


Step 1: Session ID

Login to compass on a desktop device's web browser. Once you have logged in right click with your mouse and then click inspect element.

Click on the Application tab and then click on Cookies. You should see a cookie called ASP.NET_SessionId. Copy the value of this cookie. You may need to click on the 2 arrows icon to get to Application.

Once you have clicked on Application > Cookies > yourschool.compass.education find the cookie called ASP.NET_SessionId then copy and paste it's value.



Compass has, unfortunately, made some changes to how Session IDs work for better security practices. Session IDs now expire approximately every 24 hours, which means you will need to complete this step daily. We are working on finding a way to address this; please give us some time.


Step 3: School Prefix

This step is the easiest! Simply just copy and paste the prefic before .compass.education for example if my Compass URL is ambatukam-vic.compass.education, I would paste ambatukam-vic in the input field.
