Why is v3 so limited?

Compass has implemented some API changes, along with additional restrictions on scraping. As a consequence, we are compelled to employ a less conventional method to retrieve class information from your account.

Is this version of BetterCompass open source?

Nope. I am choosing not to release the source code for this method to avoid Compass patching it.
